Cryptocurrency Pair Trading Analysis

Project 08 · Healthcare · Clinical Stats
Aloe Juice Clinical Study Analysis
Statistical analysis of a randomised clinical study: 23 cancer patients, 6-week Aloe vera juice intervention. Assessed patient-reported outcomes using advanced non-parametric tests.
GLM Mixed EffectsWilcoxon TestFriedman Testscipy · statsmodels
  • Time effect significant: p<0.05 at Week 4 & 6
  • Treatment group effect: p=0.4529 (not significant)
  • Symptom scores improved 18% over study period
Time-dependent improvement in outcomes suggests natural recovery, not treatment-specific effect. Results indicate need for larger sample size (n≥60) and double-blind design to isolate Aloe vera's therapeutic effect.
